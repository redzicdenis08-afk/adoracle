# Hook Angle Reference

## Pain
Calls out a specific frustration.
Signals: stop wasting, are you tired of, mistake, wrong, broken.
Example: Stop Wasting Money on Ads That Do Not Convert

## Transformation
Promises a before/after result.
Signals: went from, increased by, % improvement, results, how we.
Example: This Fix Increased Booked Jobs by 34%

## Social proof
Uses numbers or community size.
Signals: X customers, join, trusted by, reviews, rated.
Example: Join 2000+ founders who use...

## Scarcity
Creates urgency through limited availability.
Signals: limited, only X left, this week, expires, last chance.
Example: Limited: 3 Free Ad Audits This Week

## Authority
Signals expertise or data-backed credibility.
Signals: we analyzed, study, #1, proven, certified.
Example: We analyzed 500 campaigns...
